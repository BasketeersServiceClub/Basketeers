About Us

Basketeers is a student-led service club founded to combine the spirit of sport with the power of service. From hosting charity tournaments to running youth clinics, we use basketball as a platform to empower communities and promote positive change.

Our Projects

Charity Tournament

We organized a school-wide 3v3 tournament to raise funds for local youth centers.

Youth Clinics

Free weekend clinics focused on basketball fundamentals and teamwork for underprivileged kids.

Service Fair Booth

Promoted our mission and recruited new volunteers at the school-wide service fair.

Founder’s Note

I’m DJ Lee, the founder of Basketeers. This club started from my belief that basketball could be more than a game — it could be a tool for change. As we grew, so did our impact. I’ve led events, built partnerships, and seen the power of youth-led service firsthand. This is just the beginning.
